About the company

Zafar Technology Holding invests in startups that occupy leading positions in their industries and in talented entrepreneurs with innovative global thinking ready to implement their own ideas with responsibility and enthusiasm in the future. We allocate investment capital in technology companies open to interesting ideas, so we are not biased towards specific industries or geography.

The main advantages of Zafar Technology are professionalism, global approach, focus, customer orientation and independence. The teams consist of experts with extensive experience, professional expertise and a well-established reputation.
