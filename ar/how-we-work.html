Your path to success with Zafar Technology:

Tell what kind of business you are planning or already developing.
Together we will go through all the stages from the prototype to the IPO.

1

Concept

If you have an interesting idea, describe it in a convenient way, and we will review it even if the product has not yet been tested at the market

2

Prototype

If you have a team and the product can already be presented to the customer, we can consider your offer by adding not only funding, but also expert evaluation, necessary competencies, assistance in operational management or entering a new market, so you will get “smart-money”

3

Seed stage

If your product has a ready-made prototype and initial sales, but there is a need to find the best solution for quick scaling, realization and total completion, we will review your offer and help you bring your business to the stage of self-sufficiency and profitability

4

Revenue stage

If you are a stable company that need investment for rapid development, start global scaling of the product to enter other regions or the mass market, we will review your project and help you gain a significant market share

5

Business expansion stage

If your company has achieved certain development indicators, successfully passed several launch stages, develops infrastructure, and strives for greater growth indicators, we will review your project and help you transform your company to independent development without external support
